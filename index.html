How It Works

Three steps to your first harvest.

1

Choose Your Space

Windowsill, balcony, backyard, or indoor setup — every space can produce food. Start with what you have.

2

Pick Your Crops

Match plants to your light, space, and experience level. Herbs and greens are ready in weeks. Tomatoes and peppers take a season.

3

Grow & Harvest

Follow seasonal timing, water consistently, and harvest at peak ripeness. Build your system over time.

Why Home Harvest

The case for growing your own food.

Fresh & Nutrient-Dense

Harvest at peak ripeness when nutrient density is highest — not weeks before it hits the shelf.

No Pesticides

You control what goes on your food. No synthetic sprays. No chemical residues. Just clean produce.

Real Savings

A single packet of herb seeds replaces months of store-bought bundles. Microgreens cost pennies per tray.

Supply Resilience

When supply chains wobble, your garden doesn't. Even a fraction of home-grown food reduces dependency.

Herbs

Microgreens

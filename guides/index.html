Crop Guides

Practical growing guides matched to your experience level. Pick a crop and follow the steps.

Beginner

Herbs

Basil, cilantro, mint, parsley. Harvest in 3–4 weeks. Grow in any container on any windowsill.

3–4 weeks to harvestContainer friendly

Microgreens

Sunflower, radish, broccoli, pea shoots. Ready in 7–14 days. Dense nutrition in minimal space.

7–14 days to harvestIndoor

Lettuce & Greens

Cut-and-come-again varieties keep producing for weeks. Harvest outer leaves, the plant keeps growing.

30–45 days to harvestContainer friendly

Tomatoes

Cherry and patio varieties in containers. Need sun and consistent watering, enormous flavor payoff.

60–80 days to harvestHigh yield

Peppers

Sweet or hot. Peppers love containers and warm, sunny spots. One plant produces dozens per season.

70–90 days to harvestHigh yield

Root Vegetables

Radishes, carrots, beets. Direct sow in deep containers or beds. Radishes are ready in 25 days.

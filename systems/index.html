Growing Systems

Every method has trade-offs. Compare cost, complexity, and yield to find the system that fits your space and budget.

Soil Beds

Raised or in-ground beds. The most forgiving system for beginners with outdoor space.

Hydroponics

Soil-free growing in water and nutrients. Faster growth, higher yield, more upfront setup.

Advanced $$$$

Vertical Towers

Stack plants upward when floor space is limited. Tower gardens, wall planters, and stackable pots.

Intermediate $$$

Containers

Pots, grow bags, and buckets. Portable, affordable, and works on any surface with drainage.
